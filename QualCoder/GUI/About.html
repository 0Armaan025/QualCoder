About QualCoder

Version:

QualCoder 1.1 2019 March

Depends on Python 3.x, PyQt5, lxml, qpdf, Pillow, vlc

Tested on: Linux Mint 18, Ubuntu 18

Partly tested on: Lubuntu, Windows 10
